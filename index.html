How to Use

You use the number of dungeons scale to select how many dungeons you did in total.

You then select individual tier and chest quality for said dungeon, afterward you also select raid quality and missing people.

  • Dungeon Quality: Was your raid good or bad? 0 = a good raid, 5 = a bad raid. The fee will be reduced slightly if you increase this number, with a decently high reduction at 5.
  • Missing People: How many people were missing for said dungeon. The fee will be increased to accommodate the missing people and make it fair.

Lastly, you click on "Calculate Fees" and the total fees for each dungeon + double fee will show up.
